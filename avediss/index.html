About Avediss Gold

Founded in 2003, Avediss Gold is inspired by timeless elegance and undeniable charm of precious gold jewelry...

Services offered by a Jewelry Atelier:

Our skilled designers work closely with you to create one-of-a-kind pieces that reflect your unique style and story. From concept to creation, we ensure every detail is perfect.

Preserve the beauty and value of your treasured pieces with our expert repair and restoration services. Whether it’s fixing a broken clasp, resizing a ring, or restoring a vintage item, our experienced jewelers handle each piece with the utmost care.

Add a personal touch to your jewelry with our engraving services. Whether it’s a meaningful date, a special message, or a unique symbol, our precise engraving adds sentimental value to your cherished pieces, making them truly yours.

Keep your jewelry looking its best with our professional cleaning and maintenance services. We use safe and effective techniques to restore the sparkle and shine of your favorite pieces, ensuring they remain as beautiful as the day you got them.

Receive expert advice and personalized recommendations with our jewelry consultation services. Whether you’re looking for the perfect gift, planning a special purchase, or seeking styling tips, our knowledgeable staff is here to assist you.

Transform old or unworn jewelry into stunning new pieces with our redesign and upcycling services. We reimagine and recreate your existing items, giving them a fresh and modern look while preserving their sentimental value.

Lifetime Warranty

We guarantee that every item is free from manufacturing defects and will stand the test of time.

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Our hassle-free return policy ensures you love every piece you own.
